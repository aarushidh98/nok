WHO WE ARE—

At Nok, we offer tattoos, piercings, and tooth gems, crafted with intention, precision, and care. Derived from the Hindi word for a sharp tip, Nok represents the point where an idea meets the skin, clean, exact, and deeply personal. Experience the art of precision at Nok. Beyond the craft, Nok is a space built on feeling. We believe the process matters just as much as the final piece, with every design beginning in conversation and shaped with devotion and professionalism. Every detail is handled with care, without rushing what is meant to last. What truly sets Nok apart is the environment we have created. It is warm, honest, and human. We talk, we laugh, and we share stories. That sense of comfort and familiarity naturally extends to everyone who walks through our doors. Because it is not just about the result, it is about how you feel while you are here.

At Nok, you are seen, heard, and welcomed exactly as you are.
