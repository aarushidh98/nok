About The Edupreneurial Visionary

Redefining Learning for the Digital Age

With over 15 years at the nexus of education, tech., and entrepreneurship, Tijay Gupta has dedicated his career to dismantling obsolete learning models. His ventures blend pedagogical expertise with cutting-edge technology to create adaptive, human-centered educational ecosystems.

Called "The Architect of Learning Revolutions" by Education Weekly, Tijay's work spans AI-driven personalized learning platforms, teacher empowerment initiatives, and policy frameworks adopted across 8 countries. He believes education is the most powerful design challenge of our time.
