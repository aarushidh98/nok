ABOUT US

ABOUT US

At NOK, we create body art that feels professional and intentional. Our studio is a welcoming space where creativity, comfort, and PROFESSIONALISM come together, so every experience feels effortless and meaningful.

Our Passion

We're passionate about turning ideas, emotions, and moments into art you carry with you. every piece, big or small, is crafted with care, creativity, and attention to detail.

Hygiene and Safety

Your safety comes first at 'Nok'. we follow strict hygiene standards, use sterile single-use equipment, and maintain a clean studio environment at all times.

Your Story, Our Canvas

your body is our canvas, your story our inspiration. you imagine it - we bring it to life with art that feels authentic and timeless.

ABOUT OUR SERVICES

ABOUT OUR SERVICES

At NOK BY NANDS Studio, we're dedicated to providing top-notch tattoo and piercing services that cater to your individuality and style. Our artists and piercers are committed to delivering exceptional artistry and professionalism. Whether you're looking to express yourself through ink or adorn your body with a new piercing, we're here to make your experience extraordinary.

TATTOO

Custom tattoos designed around you. From minimal fine-line pieces to bold, expressive designs, every tattoo at NOK is created with precision, creativity, and deep attention to detail—so it feels personal, timeless, and uniquely yours.

PIERCING

Professional piercings done with the highest hygiene standards and quality jewelry. We focus on safe techniques, accurate placement, and proper aftercare to ensure a smooth healing process and a comfortable experience.

TOOTH GEM

A subtle way to elevate your smile, our tooth gems are non-invasive, safe, and carefully applied for a clean, polished finish. Perfect for those who appreciate minimal yet striking details, they add effortless charm and refined sparkle.
