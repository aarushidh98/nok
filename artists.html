NANDSSS

NANDSSS

As the "CEO and tattoo artist", Nandini is the force that keeps everything moving and evolving.

At NOK, she is constantly exploring new ways to express art.

Her work leans into meaningful, cute tattoos that carry charm without trying too hard. There is a quiet confidence in her designs that makes even the tiniest pieces feel complete. She thrives on experimentation, always pushing herself to see what else is possible.

Around her, the energy is never too serious for too long. Dad jokes slip in effortlessly, followed by just the right amount of sarcasm to keep things real.

She finds her rhythm in spaces filled with creative minds and shared stories. Conversations turn into ideas, and ideas slowly turn into art. And somewhere in that mix of humour and curiosity, she builds a space that feels alive.

PRATHAM

PRATHAM

At Nok, Pratham brings a calm that you feel the moment you sit with him.

As our resident "senior artist", his approach is rooted in patience, clarity, and craft.

He draws deeply from nature, leaning into everything serene and quietly artistic. His lines are some of the sharpest you will come across, clean, precise, and confidently placed. There is a level of control in his work that makes every detail feel just right.

What truly sets him apart is his ability to understand your idea beyond words. He takes what you imagine and shapes it into something that belongs on your skin.

Inside the studio, he also guides our students through a thoughtfully built tattoo handbook. There is a steady, big brother energy about him that instantly puts people at ease. And in that comfort, clients find the space to open up, trust the process, and see their ideas come to life.

PRATIBHA

PRATIBHA

At Nok, Pratibha brings a kind of energy you notice before you even see her work. A fashion enthusiast at heart, her world is built on colour, texture, and self expression.

As our resident "stick and poke artist", she leans into the beauty of slow, precise craftsmanship.

Her specialty lies in clean hand poke line work that feels soft yet striking on the skin. She also creates delicate lithograph tattoos that carry an almost vintage, storybook charm. Beyond the tattoo station, she is the voice shaping NOK’s presence online as our social media manager.

Her aesthetic is loud in the best way, full of bold choices and unapologetic style. Think vibrant colours, ever changing hair, and combat boots that complete the look. There is a sense of playfulness in everything she creates, from ink to digital to everyday life. And somewhere between cute art and quiet precision, she makes her mark in a way that stays with you.
